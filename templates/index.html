Constituency

A geographic area whose voters elect one representative to parliament or assembly.

EVM

Electronic Voting Machine — tamper-proof device used to cast and store votes digitally.

VVPAT

Voter Verified Paper Audit Trail — prints a paper slip confirming your vote for 7 seconds.

Lok Sabha

Lower house of India's parliament. 543 seats elected directly by citizens every 5 years.

Rajya Sabha

Upper house. Members elected by state legislators, not directly by citizens.

Model Code of Conduct

Rules governing parties and candidates from announcement until results are declared.

Affidavit

Legal declaration candidates must file disclosing criminal records, assets and liabilities.

Exit Poll

Survey of voters leaving booths, used to predict results before official counting begins.

Manifesto

A political party's official document listing their promises and plans if elected to power.

Voter ID (EPIC)

Electoral Photo Identity Card — issued by ECI to verify identity at polling booths.
